How it reads. FRA/SEN is excellent — navy vs green are far apart in hue and read instantly. But the model has no defence against a clash: Canada #D3222A vs Morocco #C1272D are the same colour, so the two blankets are indistinguishable and the possession front vanishes. Roughly a dozen 2026 pairings collide this way (red/red, blue/blue, white/white).

PRO: honest to the source, zero manual work, usually vivid. CON: fails on clashes; some primaries are near-black (Germany #464646, France navy) and sink into the background; not colourblind-audited.
