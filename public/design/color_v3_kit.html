How it reads. The most literally "on the pitch" of all options — but kits are the worst fit for a dark canvas. Senegal's white home shirt turns their entire blanket into a pale grey wash that reads as "empty / no team" against near-black, and white is also the piece's neutral set-piece colour — a direct conflict. Many nations wear white or very light home kits. Real matches also resolve clashes with an away kit, so "the kit colour" is ambiguous (which kit?). Canada/Morocco still both red.

PRO: maximum football authenticity when kits happen to be dark & distinct. CON: white/light kits vanish and collide with the neutral colour; away-kit ambiguity; still clashes.
